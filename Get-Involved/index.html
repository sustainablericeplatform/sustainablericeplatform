Become a member

As a global multi-stakeholder initiative, the Sustainable Rice Platform welcomes institutions from the public and private sectors, international agencies and development partners, research institutes, non-profits and producer groups.

Organizations can participate in the Sustainable Rice Platform by committing themselves formally to contribute, either financially or in-kind.

For membership application, please submit a completed membership application form to Secretariat@sustainablerice.org

SRP members commit themselves to:

  • Support the mission, objectives, and activities of the SRP;
  • Contribute financially and/or in kind according to their resources and institutional capacity;
  • Develop and implement within their own organizations plans of action to support the SRP’s mission and objectives;
  • Actively and constructively communicate and support the SRP’s process and implementation of projects, while adhering to SRP visibility guidelines;

An SRP institutional member can:

  • Play a leadership role in addressing critical global challenges
  • Access innovative tools and partnerships
  • Participate in collaborative projects and link with other members
  • Access technical support for field implementation of the Sustainable Rice Platform Standard
  • Leverage the outcomes of collaborative research
  • Align organizational sustainability commitments with farmer needs
  • Contribute to the UN Sustainable Development Goals
