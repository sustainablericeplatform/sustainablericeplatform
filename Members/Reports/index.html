REPORTS

Annual Plenary Meeting and General Assembly

Each year, the SRP holds an annual plenary meeting and general assembly to review its activities and plan forward. This event brings together SRP partners from governments, private sector value chain actors, research institutions, international organizations and civil society to discuss collaborative approaches and innovative solutions towards rice sustainability.
