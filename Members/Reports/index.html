Annual Plenary Meeting and General Assembly

The Sustainable Rice Platform holds an annual plenary meeting and general assembly to review its activities and plan forward. This event brings together members and partners from governments, private sector value chain actors, research institutions, international organizations and civil society to discuss collaborative approaches and innovative solutions towards rice sustainability.

2017: "First Global Sustainable Rice Conference and Exhibition" (Thailand)

2016: "Upscaling SRP: Towards Rice Sector Transformation" (Singapore)

2015: "World's First Rice Sustainability Standard Launch" (Philippines)

2014: "The Future of Rice" (Thailand)

2013: "Rice for Tomorrow" (Thailand)
