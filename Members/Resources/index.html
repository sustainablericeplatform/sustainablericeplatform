SRP STANDARD

The Sustainable Rice Platform (SRP) promotes resource-use efficiency and sustainability in the global rice sector through an alliance that links research, production, policy making, trade and consumption. It bridges institutional members across regions and sectors to find local solutions for rice smallholders that will help improve yield, reduce costs and protect the environment.

The SRP aims to encourage one million farmers to adopt climate-smart sustainable best practices in five years (2016-2020), as an active response to the global call to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity for all, as set out in the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

THE SRP PERFORMANCE INDICATORS

  • The Standard is complemented by a set of 12 quantitative Performance Indicators. By identifying ‘hotspots’, the Indicators enable users to monitor impacts of adoption of climate-smart best practices- as well as other field interventions such as training.
  • These tools are being used to develop an evidence base to measure the effectiveness of recommended practices, and also as a basis to develop sector-wide benchmarks.
  • Together, the SRP Standard and Indicators offer an objective ‘working definition’ of sustainability thatcan serve as a normative basis for M&E and policy-making as well as a benchmark for supply chain assurance schemes.
