WELCOME

Dear SRP members,

I am pleased to welcome you to the Members’ Area – where you may find useful and essential organizational materials, with some that are exclusively available for SRP members only.

I hope that these materials can help support you strengthen your own sustainability initiatives, and consequently help you contribute to the overall goal of encouraging one million farmers to adopt climate-smart sustainable best practices by 2021.

On behalf of the SRP Advisory Committee, I would like to thank you for your continued commitment to supporting SRP’s mission of promoting resource-use efficiency and sustainability in the global rice sector.

Together, let us continue working towards a sustainable future for rice, while protecting the environment, mitigating climate change impacts and safeguarding smallholder livelihoods.

Wyn Ellis

SRP Coordinator

MEMBERS

The Sustainable Rice Platform (SRP) is a global multi-stakeholder initiative to promote resource efficiency and sustainability in the global rice sector through an alliance that links research, production, policy making, trade and consumption.

Today, the SRP has expanded to include over 60 institutional members from the public and private sectors, international agencies and development partners, research institutes, non-profits and producer groups.
