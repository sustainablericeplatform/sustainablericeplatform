WELCOME

Dear SRP members,

I am pleased to welcome you to the Members’ Area – where you may find essential organizational materials, with some that are exclusively available for Sustainable Rice Platform members.

I hope that these materials can support you in strengthening your own sustainability initiatives, and consequently allow you to contribute to our overall goal of encouraging one million farmers to adopt climate-smart sustainable best practices by 2021.

On behalf of the Advisory Committee, I would like to thank you for your continuing commitment to supporting SRP’s mission of promoting resource-use efficiency and sustainability in the global rice sector.

Together, let us continue working towards a sustainable future for rice, while protecting the environment, mitigating climate change impacts and safeguarding smallholder livelihoods.


Wyn Ellis, Ph.D.

SRP Coordinator

Title: SRP 7th Annual Plenary Meeting and General Assembly

The SRP 7th Annual Plenary Meeting and General Assembly will be held on 3 October 2017 at the UN Conference Centre in UN Building, Rajdamnern Nok Ave., Bangkok, Thailand.

Each year, the SRP brings together its members and partners to discuss progress, and collaborative approaches and innovative solutions to address critical sustainability challenges facing the rice sector.

Representatives of SRP members are invited to contact the SRP Secretariat for attendance confirmation.

Posting Period: 1-30 September 2017

Title: SRP Standard and Performance Indicators Revision

The review and revision of the SRP Standard and Performance Indicators is a priority activity led by SRP Working Group 3: Farmer Support, Performance Assessment and Assurance. A total of 34 representatives of SRP members and external stakeholders were at the workshop to review, discuss and improve the SRP Standard and Performance Indicators. The consolidated outputs from the workshop will serve as basis for the next round of 60-day public stakeholder consultation, which is expected to start by 15 September. Key outcomes will be presented at the SRP 7th Annual Plenary Meeting and General Assembly.
