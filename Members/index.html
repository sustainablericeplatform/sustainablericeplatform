WELCOME

Dear SRP members,

I am pleased to welcome you to the Members’ Area – where you may find essential organizational materials, with some that are exclusively available for Sustainable Rice Platform members.

I hope that these materials can support you in strengthening your own sustainability initiatives, and consequently allow you to contribute to our overall goal of encouraging one million farmers to adopt climate-smart sustainable best practices by 2021.

On behalf of the Advisory Committee, I would like to thank you for your continuing commitment to supporting SRP’s mission of promoting resource-use efficiency and sustainability in the global rice sector.

Together, let us continue working towards a sustainable future for rice, while protecting the environment, mitigating climate change impacts and safeguarding smallholder livelihoods.


Wyn Ellis, Ph.D.

SRP Coordinator
