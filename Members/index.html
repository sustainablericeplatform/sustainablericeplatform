WELCOME

Dear SRP members,

I am pleased to welcome you to the Members’ Area – where you may find essential organizational materials, with some that are exclusively available for SRP members only.

I hope that these materials can support you in strengthening your own sustainability initiatives, and consequently allow you contribute to the overall goal of encouraging one million farmers to adopt climate-smart sustainable best practices by 2021.

On behalf of the SRP Advisory Committee, I would like to thank you for your continuing commitment to supporting SRP’s mission of promoting resource-use efficiency and sustainability in the global rice sector.

Together, let us continue working towards a sustainable future for rice, while protecting the environment, mitigating climate change impacts and safeguarding smallholder livelihoods.

Wyn Ellis

SRP Coordinator

Title: Pavilion of Partnerships for Sustainable Development, 29-31 March 2017

Venue: United Nations Conference Center, Bangkok, Thailand

The Sustainable Rice Platform will be participating as an exhibitor at the Pavilion of Partnerships during the Asia-Pacific Forum on Sustainable Development 2017 in 29-31 March 2017 at the UN Conference Centre, Bangkok, Thailand. The event is an inclusive intergovernmental forum preparatory to the annual High-Level Political Forum.

As a participant, the SRP will have an opportunity to raise awareness and visibility for its programmes and member institutions.

MEMBERS

The SRP has grown rapidly since its inception in 2011, and now comprises over 60 institutional members spanning a wide range of actors both within and outside the value chain.

SRP’s current membership includes government agencies and research institutes, rice traders and environmental and social NGOs.
