#ChooseSustainableRice

Your voice matters - commit to #ChooseSustainableRice and share your rice story with us on Instagram (@srp_rice)!

Our online awareness campaignaims to broaden public awareness of the positive economic, environmental and social benefits of sustainable rice through the use of augmented reality (AR) on Instagram camera filters.

Through the campaign, we hope to connect with youth and online communities to promote a shift to sustainable rice production and consumption to combat hunger and climate change.
